DESIGN CONCEPT 02

DewyGlow

Project Type UI/UX Design
Role UI/UX Designer & Product Designer
Key Features Brand Identity, Product Packaging, UI/UX Design & Visual Design System
Built With Figma • Adobe Photoshop • Canva

The Challenge

Many skincare brands often feel either too clinical or visually repetitive, making it difficult for younger audiences to connect with them emotionally. Modern consumers, especially Gen Z, are drawn toward brands that feel aesthetic, relatable, and digitally engaging.

The challenge with DewyGlow was to create a skincare brand identity that felt fresh, minimal, and approachable while maintaining a premium visual appeal. The project focused on building a cohesive experience through modern UI/UX design, clean packaging, and a youthful brand aesthetic.

Architectural Highlights

The project combined soft visual aesthetics, minimal layouts, modern typography, and cohesive color systems to create a clean and premium skincare brand identity. Every interface and product design element was planned to maintain consistency across digital and product experiences.
